Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the ElevateLMS Premium platform.

How long does enterprise deployment take?

Typical deployment takes between 4 to 8 weeks, depending on the complexity of your SSO integrations and custom branding requirements. Our dedicated onboarding team will guide you through every step.

Do you support SCORM and xAPI?

Yes, ElevateLMS Premium is fully compliant with SCORM 1.2, SCORM 2004, and xAPI (Tin Can). You can seamlessly import all your existing legacy training modules.

Where is the data hosted?

Enterprise clients can choose their data residency. We offer hosting in AWS data centers across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific to ensure compliance with GDPR and local data laws.
